# AI-Based Personalized Study Planner and Academic Risk Analysis System

## 1. Introduction
In the current academic environment, students often face difficulties in managing their study time efficiently due to multiple subjects, varying difficulty levels, and approaching examinations. Most students follow generic study schedules that do not consider individual strengths, weaknesses, or exam priorities, leading to ineffective preparation and academic stress.

To address this problem, this project proposes an AI-based personalized study planner that analyzes student-specific academic data and generates an optimized daily study plan. The system also identifies subjects that pose a higher academic risk, allowing students to take corrective actions in advance.

---

## 2. Problem Statement
Students lack a systematic and personalized approach to plan their studies based on subject difficulty, exam schedule, and individual confidence levels. Existing study methods are often static and do not adapt to the student’s changing performance.

---

## 3. Objectives of the Project
- To design a system that generates a personalized daily study plan for students.
- To prioritize subjects and topics based on difficulty and exam urgency.
- To analyze academic risk using rule-based and AI techniques.
- To help students improve time management and academic performance.

---

## 4. Scope of the Project
- The system focuses on individual students.
- It uses subject-wise and topic-wise academic data.
- It provides daily study plans and risk indicators.
- Machine learning is limited to basic prediction models.

---

## 5. Limitations
- The system does not guarantee academic success.
- Predictions depend on the accuracy of user-provided data.
- External factors such as personal health or environment are not considered.
